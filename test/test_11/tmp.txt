Act One

[Enter Alice.]
[Enter Bob.]

Alice.
Hello Bob!

Bob.
Hello Alice!

Alice.
It's a beautiful day.

Bob.
Indeed it is.

[Exit Alice.]

Act Two

[Enter Charlie.]

Bob.
Charlie, have you seen Alice?

Charlie.
No, I haven't.

Bob.
She went home.

Charlie.
Oh, I see.

[Exit Charlie.]
[Exit Bob.]

Act Three

[Enter Dave.]
[Enter Eve.]

Dave.
Where is everyone?

Eve.
They all left.

Dave.
This place is empty!

Eve.
Just the two of us now.

[Exit Eve.]
[Exit Dave.]
